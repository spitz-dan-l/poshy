Offline Alchemy Workbench

Poshy Lab

This file is fully self-contained. Open it directly from disk, edit your inventory, shop list, and recipes, then try crafting or buying potions one step at a time without running a server.

Persistence Auto-saves in your browser
Your edited data and current workbench are stored with localStorage on this machine.
Flow Edit rules, then test combinations
Recipe and shop edits affect the workbench immediately. Base inventory edits apply when you reset or re-seed the workbench.
Single-file mode: no build step, no dependencies, no server.
